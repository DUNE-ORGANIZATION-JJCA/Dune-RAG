## Combat Rules

1. Attackers choose battleground location
2. Both players roll combat dice simultaneously
3. Highest total wins ties
4. Defenders can retreat before combat
5. Winner chooses: capture territory OR harvest spice

## Advanced Rules

- **DesertStorm**: Moves 1-3 territories each round
- **Sandworms**: Can destroy any unit in desert
- **SpiceBlow**: Randomly adds spice to territories
- **WeaponFacilities**: Upgrades units with technology
