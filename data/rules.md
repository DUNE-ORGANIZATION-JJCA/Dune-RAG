# Dune: Arrakis Dominion - Rules Guide

## Overview

Dune: Arrakis Dominion is a strategic board game set in the Dune universe. Players compete for control of the spice-rich planet Arrakis.

## Win Conditions

1. **Capture 5 Strongholds** - Control 5 of the 7 major strongholds
2. **Harvest 100 Spice** - Accumulate 100 spice units
3. **Eliminate All Opponents** - Destroy all enemy factions

## Factions

### House Atreides
- **Bonus**: +1 combat die in desert terrain
- **Special**: Can recruit Fremen allies
- **Weakness**: Limited military units

### House Harkonnen  
- **Bonus**: +2 combat dice in urban terrain
- **Special**: Can sabatoge spice extraction
- **Weakness**: High unit cost

### House Corrino
- **Bonus**: Diplomatic immunity
- **Special**: Can buy any technology
- **Weakness**: Starts with fewer resources

## Turn Structure

1. **Storm Phase** - Calculate storm movement
2. **Spice Phase** - Harvest and collect spice
3. **Action Phase** - Move units, attack, or defend
4. **Battle Phase** - Resolve combat
5. **Revenu Phase** - Collect taxes and fees
